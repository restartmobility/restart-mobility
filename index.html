Our mission

A practical bridge between unused equipment and urgent need.

Many mobility items in the UK are left unused, while people with disabilities in Romania struggle to access basic support. Restart Mobility exists to collect, check, transport and deliver equipment with dignity and transparency.

How we help

Simple, direct, human support.

We focus on equipment that can immediately improve independence, safety and daily life.

🤝

Equipment donations

Wheelchairs, walking frames, crutches, hospital beds, hoists and other mobility aids.

🚚

Transport & delivery

Organised collection in the UK and responsible delivery to verified recipients in Romania.

👥

Family support

Helping people apply for equipment and connecting families with practical guidance.
