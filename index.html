About ReStart Mobility

ReStart Mobility is a non-profit initiative dedicated to supporting people with physical disabilities, reduced mobility, and vulnerable individuals in Zimnicea and Teleorman County, Romania.

Our mission is to provide access to essential mobility and medical equipment through the collection, refurbishment, and redistribution of donated equipment from the United Kingdom.

We believe that every person deserves the opportunity to live with independence, dignity, and equal access to mobility regardless of their financial circumstances.

Our Vision

A community where no person with a disability or reduced mobility is left without access to the equipment they need to live independently, safely, and with dignity.

About ReStart Mobility CIC

ReStart Mobility CIC is a UK-registered Community Interest Company dedicated to improving the lives of people with physical disabilities.

We collect donated mobility equipment throughout the United Kingdom and redistribute it to individuals and families in Romania who cannot access the support they need.

Our mission is to restore independence, dignity and hope through mobility.

Founded by Mihaela Cotruta

After many years working in the care sector, Mihaela founded ReStart Mobility CIC to help people with disabilities gain access to essential mobility equipment and improve their quality of life.

No person should lose their independence because they cannot access basic mobility support.
