How our social model works

We combine practical support with a sustainable social-enterprise model so that every donation, purchase and partnership can help more people.

🎁
We collect donated equipment

We source wheelchairs, hoists, beds and other mobility equipment across the UK.

🛒
We purchase when needs are urgent

Where a suitable donation is unavailable, we aim to buy the equipment a person urgently needs.

🔧
We inspect and refurbish

Equipment is cleaned, checked and prepared responsibly before reuse.

♿
We provide affordable equipment in the UK

Selected refurbished equipment is offered at prices below typical market rates, with flexible options considered where appropriate.

💚
Every surplus is reinvested

Income supports people who cannot afford essential mobility equipment in the UK and Romania.

Meet the Founder

Mihaela Cotruta

Founder & Director | ReStart Mobility CIC
I founded ReStart Mobility because I believe that access to essential mobility equipment should never depend on a person's ability to pay.

For 19 years, I have dedicated my career to supporting people with physical disabilities, complex care needs and life-changing conditions.

Throughout those years, I have witnessed how the right mobility equipment can restore independence, reduce isolation and give people the confidence to live with dignity.

These experiences inspired me to establish ReStart Mobility CIC, a UK Community Interest Company committed to collecting, purchasing, refurbishing and delivering essential mobility equipment to those who need it most.

Our mission goes beyond providing equipment. We are building a sustainable social enterprise that reinvests every surplus into helping people with disabilities across the United Kingdom and Romania.
